Recent college grad

In my free time I like to learn new skills, spend time with friends, and play video games. I have been trying to get more practical knowledge about front and back end development along with advanced java and mysql. I am trying to learn something new everyday and have fun doing it.

I grew up in Minneapolis and I love Minnesota even if it can get a little cold sometime.
